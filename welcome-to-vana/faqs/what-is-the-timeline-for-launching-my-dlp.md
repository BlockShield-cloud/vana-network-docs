# What is the timeline for launching my DLP?

* Testnet Phase: Start by developing and testing your DLP on Vana's testnet.
* Mainnet Launch: Transition to the mainnet once your DLP meets all performance and community support criteria.

Approximate Timeline:

* Initial Development: 1-2 weeks
* Testing on Testnet: 1-2 weeks
* Mainnet Launch Preparation: 1-2 weeks
